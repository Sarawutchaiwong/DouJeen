# PRODUCT: Chinese Discovery Game

## The Soul
"Learn Chinese through discovery, not memorization."

## Audience
- **Primary:** Gen Z learners who find traditional methods boring.
- **Secondary:** Puzzle game enthusiasts and curious explorers of language.

## Brand Lane: "Kawaii Alchemist"
The project lives at the intersection of a cozy crafting game and a high-fidelity educational tool. It should feel like a laboratory of discovery where every combination is a tiny "Aha!" moment.

## Voice & Tone
- **Encouraging:** "You're getting closer!"
- **Playful:** "What happens if we mix 'Fire' and 'Mountain'?"
- **Clean but Cute:** Minimalist layout with expressive, bubbly interactions.

## Core Loop
1. **Combine:** Drag and drop characters together.
2. **Discover:** See if they form a new word/concept (e.g., 火 + 山 = 火山 / Volcano).
3. **Collect:** Build a library of discovered knowledge.

## Anti-References
- **No Grids of Doom:** Avoid the "Anki" list look.
- **No Competitive Stress:** No timers or leaderboards that prioritize speed over understanding.
- **No Corporate Gray:** Avoid dull, "educational software" palettes.
