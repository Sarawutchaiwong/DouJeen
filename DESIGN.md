# DESIGN: Chinese Discovery Game

## Visual Identity: "Bubbly Exploration"

## Typography
- **Primary (Display):** Rounded Sans-Serif (e.g., "Quicksand" or "Nunito") for a friendly, approachable feel.
- **Chinese Characters:** Noto Sans SC (Bold) for clarity and modern feel.
- **Body:** Clean, highly legible sans-serif with generous line-height.

## Color Palette: "Pastel Laboratory"
- **Primary (Discovery):** Soft Mint (`#B2F2BB`) - the color of new growth.
- **Secondary (Achievement):** Warm Peach (`#FFD8A8`) - for successful combinations.
- **Background:** Off-white/Cream (`#FFF9DB`) - easy on the eyes for long sessions.
- **Action:** Vibrant Sky Blue (`#74C0FC`) - for interactive elements.

## Spatial System & Shapes
- **Organic Corners:** `border-radius: 24px` minimum for all cards and containers.
- **Airy Layout:** Heavy use of whitespace to prevent cognitive overload.
- **Depth:** Soft shadows (`box-shadow: 0 8px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`) to make elements feel "tappable."

## Component Archetypes
1. **The Character Bubble:** A rounded tile containing a single character, floating slightly.
2. **The Alchemy Zone:** A central, softly glowing area where characters are combined.
3. **The Discovery Toast:** A celebratory pop-up when a new word is found, using a playful animation.

## Interaction Design
- **Tactile Feedback:** Subtle "bounce" animations on drag and hover.
- **Sound (Mental):** Interactions should feel "plonky" and "soft."
